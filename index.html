BIO

Anade is a 31 year old Spaniard with a background in classical music and computer programming. He’s always played the Piano but it wasn’t until 2 years ago, after joining London Sound Academy as a DJ, that his career in electronic music started. As a DJ, he’s already played on some major venues around Spain and the UK, like «Industrial Copera» and «Ministry of Sound». As a producer, his music combines a deep melodic style with groove and punchy basslines. Some of his influences are: Tangerine Dream, Stephan Bodzin, Boris Brejcha, Jean Michel Jarre and Enrico Sangiuliano.
